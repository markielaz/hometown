Sports & Leisure Activities

Between the Mersey and the Dee, there's so much to do and see!

Football Clubs

  • Tranmere Rovers
    The only true Wirral football club, based at Prenton Park. Despite being a Liverpool fan, I attended quite a few home games at Tranmere due to the welcoming atmosphere and cheaper price tickets!

  • Liverpool
    Across the mersey, you'll find 2 more prestigous football clubs. Anfield's famous European nights are the stuff of legend, but it can be hard to get a ticket!

  • Everton
    Even though I'm a red, I've been lucky enough to visit Goodison Park several times. It's a real old-fashioned stadium and you can feel the timbers shake when the crowd roars.

Sailing and watersports

  • West Kirby Marine Lake.
    Ness is open all year round with seasonal flowers, shrubs and trees

Royal Liverpool Golf Club Hoylake

  • Over 150 years of heritage.
    Founded in 1869, now world famous, with Royal status granted due to patronage.

  • Host to the Open and the Walker Cup
    The first international matches between England and Scotland, and later Great Britain and the United States of America, were played here.

  • The home of amateur golf.
    Although primarily a members club, the course welcomes visitors as well.
